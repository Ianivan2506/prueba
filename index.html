About Us

Provides nearshoring software development, engineering, and related IT services

Mexicode was formed in response to its founders’ frustration with the United States technologist market across all industries. In Mexico, there are 130,000 new computer-related degrees awarded every year. A vast majority of these graduates are unable to find a career in a technology field. Meanwhile in the United States, two-thirds of most programming jobs are unfilled every year and cost of creating software is inflating at an unsustainable rate. Mexicode has created a bridge between two markets and a new industry. We provide affordable technologist resourcing without the headaches that exist in traditional outsourcing or nearshoring.
